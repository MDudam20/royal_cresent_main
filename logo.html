About the Logo Design

Living amidst serene surroundings, mountains, and vibrant flora and fauna evokes a profound sense of peace, harmony, and connection with nature. This environment inspires feelings of calmness, clarity, and a refreshing escape from the chaos of urban life.

It nurtures a sense of gratitude, well-being, and tranquility, where the rhythm of life feels unhurried and in sync with the natural world. The constant presence of greenery and tranquil location can also spark a deep sense of awe, freedom, and contentment, fostering a lifestyle that feels rejuvenating and fulfilling

The logo's seven streaks of lines symbolize the harmonious balance of the core elements that define our Bungalow Project.
